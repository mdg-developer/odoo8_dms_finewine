Select Your Criteria(Report By Warehouses)

Navigate to inventory > Reports > Inventory Report.

Select option to group "By Warehouse".

Sample Report By Warehouse(PDF).

When you select a report by warehouse, You will get a detail report about all quantity by warehouses.


* Beginning Quantities,
* Recieved Quantities,
* Sold Quantities,
* Internal Quantities,
* Ending Quantities,
* Total By Warehouses.

* Ending Quantities,
* Total By Category.
